Web3 in 2025

Exploring the future of decentralized technologies and digital assets

This project investigates the evolving landscape of Web3 technologies, focusing on the intersection of blockchain, NFTs, and digital identity. Through research and experimentation, I explore how decentralized technologies are reshaping creative industries and digital asset ownership.

The work examines emerging trends in the Web3 space, from smart contracts and DAOs to digital art markets and virtual worlds. By analyzing current developments and projecting future possibilities, this project serves as both documentation and speculation about the next phase of the internet.

Running

Exploring movement, endurance, and personal growth through running

This project documents my journey with running as both a physical practice and a mental discipline. Through tracking routes, analyzing performance data, and reflecting on the meditative aspects of long-distance running, I explore how movement can become a form of creative expression and personal development.

The work combines data visualization with personal narrative, creating visual representations of running routes, pace variations, and the emotional landscape of endurance sports. Each run becomes a data point in a larger story of growth, challenge, and achievement.

Technologies

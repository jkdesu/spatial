I am an aspiring brand designer with a background in Economics from Waseda University, Tokyo. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computational Design at Columbia University's GSAPP, where I explore the intersection of culture, value, and emotion through visual identity and data visualization.

While living in Tokyo, I was drawn to the cultural scene through my passion for collecting designer figures and hypewear, which inspired me to shift away from a traditional path in finance—despite having held multiple finance internships—and pursue a creative career.

My current interests lie in the application of NFTs, the allocation of digital assets across blockchain ecosystems, and the use of computational methods and algorithms to create digital art. With roots in painting and Adobe Creative Suite, I now blend traditional and digital practices to explore new forms of expression.

Beyond design and research, I am passionate about fitness and outdoor activities, including surfing, running, tennis, and working out, as well as continuing to explore emerging digital assets.
